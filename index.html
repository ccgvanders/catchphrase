How to play
Buzzer Mode: One person holds the screen and describes the top phrase (no rhyming, no saying the word itself) so their team guesses it. Hit GOT IT to move to the next phrase and pass the device to the other team immediately. Whoever is holding the device when the buzzer sounds, the other team scores the point. SKIP a phrase if it's too tricky — costs time off the clock if a timer is running.
All-In Mode: Take turns going round the table describing phrases — everyone else guesses at once. Whoever calls it correctly first gets the point (keep score on paper, or however you like — agree on a target score to win). Hit GOT IT when someone guesses correctly to bring up the next phrase. Use a 5-minute timed round for pacing, or switch to free play for no clock at all.
