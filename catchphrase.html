How to play
One person holds the screen and reads the top word/phrase, describing it (no rhyming, no saying the word itself) so their team guesses it. When guessed, hit GOT IT — the phrase changes and the device passes to the other team immediately. Keep passing back and forth as fast as you like. Whoever is holding the device when the buzzer sounds, the other team scores the point. Hit SKIP if a card's too hard or awkward — it costs 5 seconds off the clock. Start a new round after each buzzer.
